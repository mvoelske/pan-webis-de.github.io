Participation Guide

Shared task participation:

  1. Register for a shared task.
  2. Download the task's data.
  3. Request a virtual machine on TIRA.
  4. Deploy and evaluate your software on TIRA (see Section 3.4).
  5. Write a notebook paper about your approach.
  6. Share your code on PAN's Github page.

Submission Terms

  • Authors retain copyright of their software and data.
  • Authors provide usage rights to PAN and TIRA for the purpose of publication and evaluation.
  • Authors may revoke usage rights at any time.
  • Use of TIRA is at the author's risk.
  • The choice of license rests with the authors.
  • When using a third party resource, cite its authors.
