Participation guide

  1. Register for a task and get the data.
  2. Request a virtual machine on Tira.
  3. Deploy your software. [Why submit software?]
  4. Prepare your notebook for submission in the workshop proceedings.
  5. Get in touch with us to release your code in the PAN repository: github.com/pan-webis-de

Terms for submissions

  • Authors retain copyright of their software and code.
  • Authors provide usage rights to PAN and TIRA for the purpose of evaluation.
  • Authors may revoke usage rights at any time.
  • Use of TIRA is at the author's risk.
  • The choice of license rests with the authors. Respect the license.
  • When you use some code, cite the authors. If you don't know how, ask.

Join PAN

PAN is a community-driven effort. If you want to help push forward the digital text forensics. There's a number of ways in which you can contribute.

  • Participate in a task.
  • Submit a new dataset for an existing task.
  • Organize an event.
