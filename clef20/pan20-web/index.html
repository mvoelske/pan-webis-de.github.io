This is the 20th evaluation lab on digital text forensics. PAN will be held as part of the CLEF conference in Thessaloniki, Greece, on September 22-25, 2020. Evaluations will commence from January till June. We invite you to take part in any of the tasks shown below.

Profiling Fake News Spreaders on Twitter Learn more

Given tweets of an author, determine their fake-news to real-news ratio.

Celebrity Profiling: Influencer Edition Learn more

Given tweets of followers, determine the demographics of the followee.

Cross-Domain Authorship Verification Learn more

Given two documents, determine if they are written by the same author.

Style Change Detection Learn more

Given two consecutive paragraphs, determine if there is a style change.
